The Best Movies According to Christy

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Sound of Music

This classic film has fantastic songs, exemplary acting and a marvelous story. I could watch it again and again.

Pollyanna

This movie shows how kindness can be contagious, however it is not as easily caught as complaining. This is another feel-good classis.

The Proposal

I think it is the stellar acting in this movie that keeps me coming back and the beautiful Alaskan scenary. Plu the love story is timeless.
